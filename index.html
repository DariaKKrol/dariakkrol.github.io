About me

Some time ago, I made a decision to change my profession and start a new career path as a software tester. In my previous professional work as an architect assistant and designer over the last 15 years, I gained experience working both in a team (including English-speaking teams) and independently. The nature of this profession allowed me to develop skills in planning, predicting, and analyzing. I have experience in preparing technical specifications. I am a detail-oriented person and I like to delve into the specifics.
Althought I always enjoyed architecture, over time I realized that using various tools and programs and testing their capabilities give me more fun. At some point, I started wondering if they could be done better. That's why I decided to change the direction of my career towards the IT world, specifically software testing.
